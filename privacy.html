Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2, 2025

Welcome to Oryon! This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and a unique user ID.
  • User-Generated Content: We process the images you upload to provide our AI-powered creative features.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services.
  • To personalize your experience.
  • To process in-app purchases.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase: We use Firebase for authentication, database, and hosting.
  • Google Gemini: Your uploaded images are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing to generate the creative content. We do not store your images after processing is complete.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Please contact us to make such a request.
